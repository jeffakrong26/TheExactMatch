Questions

Common questions.

Is it really free to submit? +
Yes — 100% free. There is no fee, no subscription, and no cost to get offers on your vehicle. We're compensated by the buying dealer when a transaction closes. You pay nothing.
How do I know the offers are real? +
All offers come from vetted dealer partners in our network — not automated algorithms. Real buyers, real numbers, real conversations. We only work with dealers who have a track record of closing.
What if I still owe money on my car? +
No problem — just let us know in the form. Dealers handle trade-ins with existing loans regularly. The process is the same; the dealer will pay off your remaining balance as part of the transaction.
How long do I have to accept an offer? +
Offers are typically valid for 7 days from the time they're made, though this can vary by dealer. We'll communicate the timeline clearly with each offer you receive.
