Dealer Partner Network

Partner Network Terms

These are the terms of your referral partnership with TheExactMatch. They're written in plain language on purpose — this is a working relationship between our team and yours, not a document meant to bury you in legalese. By checking the box on the partner application, you're agreeing to what's below.

Last updated July 14, 2026

1.What This Program Is

TheExactMatch refers buyers who've requested help finding a specific vehicle to partner dealers whose inventory matches. When one of your listings is the match, we route that buyer to you as a lead. You work the lead like you would any other; if it turns into a sale, a referral fee applies as described in Section 4.

2.Cost to Join

The Partner Network is free to join and free to participate in at this time — there's no signup cost, subscription, or platform fee. If that ever changes, we'll tell you directly before anything is charged. You'll never be moved onto a new cost structure without notice.

3.Speed Matters — A Lot

Our buyers come to us because they want a specific car and don't want to wait around. That urgency is the whole value of the network, and it only works if partners move fast on the leads we send.

  • Verify quickly. When you get a lead, confirm the vehicle is still available as soon as you can — ideally within the hour. The faster you verify, the better your standing in the network.
  • Don't sit on it. Leads that go unverified or unanswered for too long get rerouted to another partner in your market, and repeated non-response affects your rating and how much priority your dealership gets on future matches.
  • Keep us posted. As a lead moves through your process, update its status. We follow up with buyers in the meantime, and stale, silent leads reflect poorly on the partnership — a lead that goes fully dark on an active deal is treated as a serious miss, not a minor one.

None of this requires anything complicated — a quick reply and an honest status update is all it takes to stay in good standing.

4.Referral Fees & Payment

If a referred buyer purchases from you, a referral fee is owed to TheExactMatch under the fee terms you set on your partner application (flat dollar amount, or a percentage of sale price / front gross — whichever you chose). If your dealership didn't have a fee policy in place yet when you applied, we'll work that out with your team directly before it applies to any deal.

Fees are due within 30 days of the sale. We'd rather have it sooner — the quicker fees are settled, the smoother everything else in the partnership runs. We track every open fee and will follow up until it's paid; please don't make us chase it.

Repeated late payment or unpaid fees are grounds for pausing or ending a partnership, same as any other standing issue.

5.Customer Satisfaction

The buyer's experience with you reflects directly on us, since we're the ones who made the introduction. We check in with buyers after a referral to see how things went. A pattern of unhappy customers coming out of your leads — regardless of whether the sale closed — puts the partnership at risk. Treat a referred buyer exactly like you'd treat someone who walked onto your lot on their own.

6.Market Zone Coverage

To keep the network useful for everyone, we generally keep one active partner per market and zone (and, for franchise dealers, per brand within that zone). This is also why your zip code matters on the application — it's what determines which zone your dealership represents.

If you leave the dealership you signed up under, just email us and let us know where you're headed. We'll check whether your new dealership's market and zone are open, and let you know if the partnership can continue there.
7.Accurate Inventory

Matching only works if what's listed reflects what's actually on your lot. Keep pricing and availability current. If a car we match on turns out to already be sold or materially different from the listing, it undermines the buyer's trust in the whole process — and in repeated cases, affects your standing in the network.

8.Buyer Information Stays Between Us

Any buyer contact information we share with you is for the purpose of pursuing that specific lead — not for building a marketing list, reselling, or sharing with anyone outside your dealership.

9.Independent Businesses

TheExactMatch and your dealership are independent businesses. Nothing in this partnership creates an employment, agency, or joint-venture relationship, and referrals are not exclusive or guaranteed in volume — how many leads route to you depends on your inventory, market/zone, and standing in the network.

10.Ending the Partnership

Either side can end the partnership at any time — just let us know. We may also pause or deactivate a partner account for prolonged non-responsiveness, unpaid fees, poor buyer outcomes, or inaccurate listings. If circumstances change, you're welcome to reapply.

11.Changes to These Terms

As the Partner Network grows we may need to update these terms — most likely around fee structure, response-time expectations, or coverage rules. If we make a change that affects you, we'll notify you directly rather than updating this page silently. Continuing to accept leads after being notified means you're accepting the update.

Questions about any of this? Reach out any time at info@theexactmatch.com.
